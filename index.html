HouseholdOS

A local-first iPhone and iPad app that helps you run a household without the friction. Track bills, tasks, documents, expirations, and members — all on your device, with no account and no server.

What it does

  • Household context for bills, tasks, documents, and expirations
  • Encrypted document vault (AES-GCM, key in iOS Keychain)
  • Scheduled reminders — delivered locally by iOS, no push server
  • One-tap export and full local backup
  • Face ID / Touch ID app lock with a separate sensitive-documents mode

HouseholdOS Plus

The free tier covers one household with up to 10 bills, 20 tasks, 10 documents, and basic reminders. HouseholdOS Plus unlocks:

  • Unlimited bills, tasks, and documents
  • Advanced reminders and smarter organization
  • Premium export and backup tools
  • Future premium upgrades included

Plus is available as a monthly subscription, yearly subscription, or one-time lifetime unlock. Pricing is shown inside the app and is handled by Apple through the App Store. See Terms for subscription details.
